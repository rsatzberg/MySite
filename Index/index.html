My School

I attend the University of New Haven in Connecticut. I am a Music and Sound Recording Major with a minor in both Economics and Theater Arts. I am the Station Manager of our radio station, WNHU, where I also co-host a show called The Twist on Tuesday nights. I am also a Resident Assistant in Gerber Hall with the Music LLC. I am a rising senior, set to graduate in May of 2020.

Fun Stuff

My hobbies include listening to music, recording musicians and mixing songs.I love working with people and volunteering my time to causes I care about. I have been working with my university's theater department for the past year, working sound for shows. I recently ran the sound board for my first show, and I am the foley artist for the upcoming production of Mr. Burns: A Post Electric Play. I hope to have a career in sound for theater, or in sound engineering.
